Super Nachos


These homemade nachos are great for game day. This recipe makes a huge meal-sized tray of nachos loaded with taco-seasoned ground beef, refried beans, salsa, jalapeños, and cheese.

Ingredients

  • Seasoned ground beef
  • Shredded Cheddar cheese
  • Canned refried beans
  • Chopped tomatoes
  • Canned black olives
  • Salsa
  • Sour Cream
  • Green Onions
  • Sliced jalapeños peppers

    • Steps

    1. Cook and season the ground beef on the stove.
    2. Spread the tortilla chips on a baking sheet.
    3. Top the chips with cheese, beans, and beef.
    4. Broil until the cheese is melted.
    5. Top with olives, salsa, sour cream, green onions, and jalapeños.
